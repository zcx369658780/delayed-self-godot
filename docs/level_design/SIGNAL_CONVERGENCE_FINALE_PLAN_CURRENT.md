## 4. Primary topology contract — candidate A

### 4.1 Compact zone model

Author a compact, mutually visible three-zone loop:

1. **Setup loop:** shared spawn, recovery circulation, and the single dependency Plate `S`.
2. **Phase-transfer lane:** the single Door `D1`; crossing it changes which route/history alignment is reachable without changing any rule.
3. **Final convergence pocket:** distinct Plates `A` and `B`, final Door `D2` depending on A+B, a one-cell Door approach for YOU, and EXIT beyond `D2`.

`D1` and `D2` must be terrain articulations for their intended stage. The board should reuse sightlines: the player should be able to see the relevant Door and its labeled dependency before committing, without displaying a route.

### 4.2 Exact causal obligations

The authored geometry must make this chain true in every shortest solution:

1. a delayed actor creates a visible result on `S`, opening `D1`;
2. YOU or the causally required actor enters/passes `D1` from the following open start-turn snapshot;
3. the `D1` traversal or resulting route access changes phase/position so the final E2/E4 alignment becomes reachable;
4. E2 and E4 simultaneously occupy distinct `A` and `B` while YOU is already free in the final approach;
5. the committed result opens `D2`;
6. on the following input, YOU enters `D2` from its open start-turn snapshot;
7. YOU alone reaches EXIT.

Stage 1 must enable stage 2, not merely precede it on a corridor. Removing or bypassing `D1` must materially collapse the causal chain. The final A+B state must not occur usefully before the stage-1 obligation.

### 4.3 Role and substitution guardrails

- `A` and `B` must each be at least two traversable moves from the final Door cell, with no alternate Plate-to-Door shortcut.
- The final approach must require YOU to be free before the A+B result.
- If YOU substitutes on A or B, leaving that Plate may create at most one open result snapshot, but YOU cannot enter `D2` on the next input; the Door closes before entry.
- One remaining ECHO cannot occupy both distinct final Plates, and legal overlap presses only one cell.
- Individual E2-only and E4-only variants must be complete-unsolved when relevant to the final causal chain.
- The single Plate `S` must not be reachable or holdable in a way that lets an early action satisfy both stages accidentally.
- The selected shortest-role result should be E2/E4 on A/B in one stable mapping, but role mapping is a future proof result, not a Help disclosure.

### 4.4 Recovery and anti-padding

The setup loop must allow at least four plausible non-shortest actions to remain recoverable without restart. Each must lead to a distinct canonical successor and an auditable route back to a solvable state. Blocked cardinals or WAIT may exist under the simulation contract, but repeated collision, literal WAIT, empty corridors, and forced replay cycles do not count as decisions or recovery quality.

Target 4–6 meaningful decisions across:

- choosing the stage-1 setup branch;
- committing to the `D1` window;
- using the post-`D1` phase or lane;
- staging YOU for final freedom;
- forming A+B;
- entering `D2` on the next open snapshot.
